Overview of AI industry and models

Here is a link to a webpage with logos from a few of the current AI companies. There's near to 100 company logos here, but there are a lot more companies that are using AI in their product offerings.
The point I want to make, is that many of these companies are to some extent resellers. They may be using propietary data for training, or fine tuning the models -- or even using a variety of models -- but for the most part there are only a handful of 'big' AI providers, who are training models on billions of paramaters. https://xyz-abc-show-and-tell.github.io/pidp/3320_facilitating_online_learning_fundamentals/logo_page.html

OpenAI

  • Founded: 2016
  • Market Cap: $300B
  • Employees: 500 - 2000
  • Projected Revenue 2025: $12.7B

Generative Pre-trained Transformer architecture. Models are trained on diverse internet data and fine-tuning with reinforcement learning from human feedback.

Most recent flagship model, GPT-4o (“o” for “omni”), fast response, produces images, video, text [formely video and images required seperate models, like dal-e-3 series]

Broad consumer and developer adoption, focusing on general-purpose AI. Foundational/Technical Papers:
