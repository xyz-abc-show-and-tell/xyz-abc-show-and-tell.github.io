Saturday December the 5th

After spending 2 weeks in quarantine, Lilliana Wright and I (Yuri Tricys), drove to Nakusp from New Denver to return the U-haul truck she had rented for her move. After dropping off the truck, we drove back to Roseberry BC and had a look at the Wild Rose restaurant. Their family business ran successfully for thirty years, and was retired in 2013. Then we went for dinner at Andrea’s house, some few hundred meters from the Rose.
